Block Explorer

Search any transaction, block or address.

One search box for the whole blockchain, paste a block height, a hash, a transaction ID or any address and Bitcoin Insight figures out the rest. Then tap from block to transaction to address and back, the way the chain actually connects.

A live "Latest Blocks" feed streams new blocks the moment they're mined, with a gentle haptic when a new tip lands. And it's strictly read-only: looking up an address never adds it to your wallet and is never saved on your device.

  • Search blocks, transactions and addresses

Wallets

Watch your stack. Keep your keys.

Add an xpub, ypub or zpub to follow an entire wallet, or paste a single address. Bitcoin Insight derives your addresses on-device, adds up the balances and pulls in the full transaction history, valued live in your currency across 19 options.

It stays strictly watch-only. Paste a private key or seed phrase by accident and the app blocks it, so your keys stay yours and your coins never move.

  • xpub, ypub, zpub or a single Bitcoin address
  • Multiple wallets with a combined total balance
  • Full history of sent and received transactions
  • 100% watch-only, private keys are rejected
Free, like the rest of the app
Security & Privacy

Private by default. Locked by Face ID.

Lock the Wallet tab behind Face ID, Touch ID or Optic ID, the rest of the app stays open. Balances blur on every launch and re-hide whenever you switch away, so no one reads your net worth over your shoulder, and your wallet never even shows up in the app-switcher preview.

There are no accounts and no servers holding your data. Wallets live only in the iOS Keychain on your device, and network data comes straight from the public mempool.space API.

  • Face ID / Touch ID / Optic ID wallet lock
  • Tap-to-reveal, hide-on-launch balance blur
  • Keychain storage, no cloud, no account

FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things people ask most about Bitcoin Insight, the mempool, and watch-only wallets.

Is there an official mempool app for iPhone?
mempool.space offers its explorer on the web and as an Android app, but there's no official native mempool app for iOS. Bitcoin Insight is a native iPhone app that brings the same live mempool, fee and network data, plus a built-in block explorer, to iOS, using the public mempool.space API. It's independent and not affiliated with mempool.space.
How is it different from the mempool.space website?
Bitcoin Insight is a purpose-built native app rather than a website. You get a glanceable dashboard, finger-scrubbable charts, Home and Lock Screen widgets, watch-only wallet tracking with Face ID, and a tracking-free explorer, all tuned for iPhone. It uses mempool.space's public data as its source.
Is it free? Is there a subscription?
The app is completely free with no subscription, no accounts and no ads. The dashboard, all charts, the block explorer, watch-only wallets and every security feature are free. The only paid item is an optional one-time lifetime unlock (about €4.99) for the Home and Lock Screen widgets, pay once, yours forever.
What is a watch-only wallet, and is it safe?
A watch-only wallet tracks balances and transactions using only a public key (xpub, ypub or zpub) or a single address, never a private key. Bitcoin Insight actively rejects private keys and seed phrases, derives addresses on your device, and stores wallets in the iOS Keychain. It can never move your coins, so there's no spending risk.
What is Moscow time?
Moscow time is how many satoshis equal one unit of your fiat currency (100,000,000 ÷ the Bitcoin price). As Bitcoin rises, the number of sats per dollar or euro falls. Bitcoin Insight shows Moscow time live on the dashboard and as an interactive chart in all 19 supported currencies.
What's a good Bitcoin fee (sat/vB)?
It depends on how busy the mempool is. Bitcoin Insight works as a live fee tracker, showing low, medium and high recommendations in sat/vB plus a chart of how fees have trended, so you can pick the cheapest moment to send. When the mempool is quiet you can often pay 1–2 sat/vB; when it's congested, higher fees confirm faster.
Does it need an account or collect my data?
No. There's no sign-up or login, no analytics and no tracking. Everything you enter stays on your device, network data comes straight from the public mempool.space API, and the app is fully open source so anyone can verify it.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

No Data Collection

Bitcoin Insight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Local Storage Only

Everything you enter into the app, such as wallet addresses and preferences, is stored exclusively on your device. We don't operate any servers, so nothing is sent to us.

Third-Party API

To display Bitcoin network data and watch-only wallet balances, the app connects to the public mempool.space API. These requests are made directly from your device. When you add a watch-only wallet or look up an address in the explorer, its addresses are visible to mempool.space in order to look up balances. Please refer to the mempool.space privacy policy for details.

Purchases

The optional one-time widget unlock is processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We never receive your payment details. The Apple Privacy Policy applies.

No Cookies

Neither the app nor this website uses cookies or any other tracking technologies.
