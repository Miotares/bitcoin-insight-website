Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

No Data Collection

Bitcoin Insight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Local Storage Only

All data you enter into the app (such as wallet addresses) is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers – we don't operate any.

Third-Party API

To display Bitcoin network data, the app connects to the public mempool.space API. These requests are made directly from your device to their servers. We have no access to these requests. Please refer to the mempool.space privacy policy for details on how they handle API requests.

Apple & App Store

Bitcoin Insight is distributed via the Apple App Store. The privacy policies of Apple and the App Store apply accordingly.

No Cookies

Neither the app nor this website uses cookies or any other tracking technologies.
