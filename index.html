Features

The whole network, beautifully clear.

Every important Bitcoin metric, presented with the same calm, focused design, from the live dashboard down to the smallest detail.

Live Dashboard

Price, block height, mempool, network fees, hashrate, difficulty, Moscow time, circulating supply, halving countdown and Lightning stats, all updating in real time and refreshed with a pull.

Go Deeper

Tap any metric for the full story: block contents and rewards, the emission schedule, halving math, fee distribution and difficulty adjustments, visualized with elegant charts.

Watch-Only Wallets

Add any xpub, ypub, zpub or single address. Track balances and full transaction history, never a private key.

Home & Lock Widgets

13 widgets for price, fees, halving, block height and more, in every size including Lock Screen. The one optional add-on.

Privacy First

No accounts, no analytics, no ads. Your data stays on your device. Network data comes straight from mempool.space.

Open Source

Every line is public under the MIT license. Audit it, contribute, or build your own version.

Wallets

Watch your stack. Keep your keys.

Add an xpub, ypub or zpub to follow an entire wallet, or paste a single address. Bitcoin Insight derives your addresses, adds up the balances and pulls in the full transaction history, with live values in your currency.

It stays strictly watch-only. You share a public key, never a private one, so your keys stay yours and your coins never move.

  • xpub, ypub, zpub or a single Bitcoin address
  • Multiple wallets with a combined total balance
  • Full history of sent and received transactions
  • 100% watch-only, private keys never touched

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

No Data Collection

Bitcoin Insight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Local Storage Only

Everything you enter into the app, such as wallet addresses and preferences, is stored exclusively on your device. We don't operate any servers, so nothing is sent to us.

Third-Party API

To display Bitcoin network data and watch-only wallet balances, the app connects to the public mempool.space API. These requests are made directly from your device. When you add a watch-only wallet, its addresses are visible to mempool.space in order to look up balances. Please refer to the mempool.space privacy policy for details.

Purchases

The optional one-time widget unlock is processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We never receive your payment details. The Apple Privacy Policy applies.

No Cookies

Neither the app nor this website uses cookies or any other tracking technologies.
